# Sóró Brand Guide

This guide captures the current brand system for Sóró and should be used as the default reference for new UI, copy, motion, and illustration decisions.

## Brand Positioning

Sóró is a verified student insights platform. It connects brands to real Nigerian university students through AI-moderated conversations, then turns those conversations into fast, usable research.

The brand promise is:

- verified voices
- honest answers
- fast turnaround
- real cash for students

If a design or message does not support those four ideas, it should be questioned.

## Core Identity

The logo suggests a voice being amplified, cultural grounding, and ideas becoming signal. The brand should feel:

- Afro-centric without becoming decorative
- warm and human without feeling informal or unserious
- premium and editorial without becoming cold
- modern and digital without losing texture

The strongest brand metaphor is voice turned into insight.

## Color System

These colors are already implemented in the app and should stay the baseline palette.

### Base

- Background / canvas: `#FDFBF2`
- Foreground / text: `#1F1612`
- Card surface: `#FFFDF6`
- Border: `#E6DFCC`
- Muted surface: `#F1ECDC`
- Muted text: `#6C6358`

### Accents

- Terracotta: `#B74A26`
- Ochre: `#CFA331`
- Sage: `#7A8452`

### Recommended usage

- Terracotta: primary actions, brand emphasis, high-attention states
- Ochre: secondary emphasis, highlights, promotional details
- Sage: trust, success, verification, calm supportive details
- Cream and charcoal: the main canvas for readability and premium restraint

### Color rule

Use accent colors sparingly. The system should feel mostly grounded and neutral, with color used as a signal rather than decoration.

## Typography

The typography direction should be editorial and confident.

### Recommended hierarchy

- Headings: a high-contrast serif or a refined display face
- Body: a clean sans-serif with strong readability
- Microcopy and data labels: a restrained monospace or compact sans where needed

### Typography rules

- Headlines should feel intentional and slightly literary
- Body copy should stay direct, readable, and modern
- Avoid overusing all-caps or loud display styling
- Keep line length comfortable and spacing generous

## Voice and Tone

The brand voice should sound like a sharp, trustworthy operator, not a generic startup brochure.

### Voice traits

- clear
- human
- grounded
- confident
- locally aware

### Copy style

- say what the product does plainly
- use short, factual claims where possible
- avoid vague hype words like “revolutionary” or “game-changing”
- favor trust, proof, verification, and speed over abstract aspiration

### Useful phrase territory

- verified students
- honest answers
- real talk
- cash, not points
- fast research
- actionable insight

## UI Principles

### Layout

- Keep layouts calm, structured, and spacious
- Use strong section hierarchy
- Let one message lead each screen
- Prefer clean cards, soft borders, and restrained elevation

### Motion

- Use motion to imply speech, flow, or verification
- Keep animations subtle and purposeful
- Avoid playful motion that weakens the trust signal

### Visual texture

- A little warmth and grain is good
- Avoid sterile SaaS surfaces
- Do not overdo glassmorphism or neon treatment
- Use texture, rhythm, and spacing to carry the brand more than heavy ornament

## Do

- Use cream backgrounds with dark text for the default canvas
- Use terracotta for the main CTA
- Use ochre and sage as supporting accents
- Keep the product message centered on verified human insight
- Make trust visible through verification, structure, and clarity

## Don't

- Do not make the interface feel like a generic fintech dashboard
- Do not let the palette become decorative wallpaper
- Do not use too many accent colors at once
- Do not drift into student-gig slang or unserious messaging
- Do not lose the research credibility in pursuit of style

## Design North Star

Sóró should feel like a premium research platform built on cultural honesty: warm, credible, fast, and unmistakably human.
